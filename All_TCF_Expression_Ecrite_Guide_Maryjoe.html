📖 How to Use This Guide — Before Every Tâche

This guide is your pre-game warm-up. Before you write a single word of any tâche, open this page and follow these 4 steps:

Step 1 — Find Your TopicClick the right topic in the sidebar. Read the B2 and C1 columns.
Step 2 — Pick 3 Words to UseChoose 2 B2 upgrades and 1 C1 phrase. Write them on a scrap paper.
Step 3 — Check the A1 AlertLook at the A1 words most likely to sneak into this topic. Avoid them!
Step 4 — Write & Use ThemInclude your 3 chosen words in your tâche. Every session = new weapons!
💡Remember: B1 = where you start. B2 = your target floor. C1 = your ambition. C2 = shown for awareness. Every column has an English translation so you always know exactly what you are saying. No guessing! 🎯
