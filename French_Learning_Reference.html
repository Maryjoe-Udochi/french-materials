How to Use Tenses

  • Présent: actions happening now.
  • Passé composé: completed past actions.
  • Imparfait: descriptions, habits in the past.
  • Subjonctif: doubt, emotion, necessity, desire.
  • Conditionnel présent: would/could situations.
  • Futur simple: future actions.
  • Futur proche: going to do something.
  • Plus-que-parfait: action completed before another past action.

French Articles

  • Le = masculine singular
  • La = feminine singular
  • L' = before vowel or silent h
  • Les = plural
  • Un = a/an masculine
  • Une = a/an feminine
  • Des = some

Common Gender Clues

  • Words ending in -tion, -sion, -té are often feminine.
  • Words ending in -age, -isme, -ment are often masculine.
